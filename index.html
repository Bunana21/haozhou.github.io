Short Bio

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford , working with Prof. Michael Wooldridge and Prof. Anisoara Calinescu. Prio to this, I received my PhD, supervised by Maria Kyropoulou and Panagiotis-Kanellopoulos, at the Centre for Computational Finance and Economic Agents (CCFEA) , University of Essex in 2023.

My research interests include algorithmic game theory, computational finance, and agent-based financial modelling. I am highly interested in reasoning about the strategic behaviours in large-scale financial networks from both game-theoretic and empirical perspectives, as well as the applications of reinforcement learning techniques in finance and economics.
